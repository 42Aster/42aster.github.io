Living forge (Ornn's passive)

Ornn can upgrade most Legendary items that he and his teammates own into Masterwork items, increasing the gold value of all upgradeable stats by exactly 1000 Gold, shared equally among them. Ornn can upgrade the first eligible Legendary item in an allied champion's inventory into its Masterwork variant for each level after 13 and before 18 (total of 4, once per ally per game). He can do so by selecting the ally while within 600 range and by performing Living Forge's channel.
